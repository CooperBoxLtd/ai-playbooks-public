# Code of Conduct

This project should remain practical, respectful, and focused on improving AI-assisted engineering practice.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on actionable engineering guidance.
- Assume contributors may come from different teams, countries, and experience levels.
- Avoid sharing confidential, customer-specific, or private material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Submitting secrets, credentials, or customer data.
- Repeated off-topic promotion or spam.
- Misrepresenting the playbooks as a certification, audit, or compliance guarantee.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate this code of conduct or the contribution rules.

For sensitive concerns, contact the project maintainers through the repository owner.
